## 📚 What is LSTM?

**LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)** is a special type of **Recurrent Neural Network (RNN)** that can learn long-term dependencies in sequential data. Introduced by Hochreiter & Schmidhuber (1997), LSTM solves the **vanishing gradient problem** faced by traditional RNNs.

## 🏗️ LSTM Architecture

An LSTM cell has **3 main gates:**

| Gate | Function |
|------|----------|
| 🚪 **Forget Gate** | Decides what info to discard |
| 🚪 **Input Gate** | Decides what new info to store |
| 🚪 **Output Gate** | Decides what to output |
